How to Order

Here is the process of ordering your wedding dress:

1️⃣ Design & Details
You choose the dress model you like. If you want any modifications (sleeves, neckline, back, train length, fabric details, etc.), tell us and we’ll confirm what can be customized.
2️⃣ Measurements
After confirmation, we send you our size chart. You simply take the measurements with your nearest tailor and send them to us. The dress is made specially to your measurements.
3️⃣ Payment
We require 50% prepayment to start production. The remaining 50% is paid before shipping.
4️⃣ Production
Sewing time is 2–8 weeks, depending on the design and customization.
5️⃣ Delivery
Delivery takes 7–14 days. We provide tracking once the dress is shipped.
Need help choosing?
Message us your wedding date + budget + style you like, and we’ll recommend the best options.
